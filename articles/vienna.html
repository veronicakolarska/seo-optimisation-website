Vienna - a beautiful classic!

The capital of Austria, Vienna is situated east of the the Danube River. The city is an unique, artistic and intellectual wonder that was shaped by the great minds of Mozart, Beethoven, Sigmund Freud and many, many more. If you are every there, there is a huge list of things that you can do.

Vienna State Opera

The Vienna State Opera is an gorgeous opera house and opera company based in the capital city of Vienna. You can witness world class artists preforming various types of performance all throughout the year.

Schonnbrunn palace and gardens

The Schonnbrunn palace was the summer residence of the Habsburg rulers during the rain of the monarchy. Since the latter half of the last century, it has become an key tourist destination in the city, with thousands of tourists visiting it each month.

Giant Ferris Wheel - the symbol of Vienna

The Prater is almost synonymous with Vienna. more than 60 meters tall, it provides an astonishing view of the city on the Danube. The structure was constructed in 1897 to celebrate the 50th anniversary of Emperor Franz Joseph's coronation.

Traditional apple strudel - the delicious Wiener apfelstrudel

The only thing that can be said here is - if you go to Vienna, do not miss out on trying there Apple Strudel! It's a worthwhile gastronomical experience.
